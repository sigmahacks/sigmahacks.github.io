An online hackathon to address the worst of global problems with the best of solutions.

With SigmaHacks 3, our team's mission was to provide hackers around the world with the resources and opportunities they needed to solve a socioeconomic problem affecting a specific region, allowing us to foster innovation, encourage diversity, and make progress in areas that are typically overshadowed and overlooked.

As with all of our events, SigmaHacks 3 featured unique experiences and opportunities available to all participants, in addition to engaging activities and thousands of dollars in both cash and technology prizes.
